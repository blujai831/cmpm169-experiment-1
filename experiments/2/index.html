This game has music. I recommend playing with the sound on.

This game is intended to be played with an external mouse. Trying to use a touchpad will pose a harsh disadvantage. A touchscreen presents unique advantages and disadvantages that are interesting in their own right, but not strictly intended.

There's more to the game than there may appear to be at first glance. Interesting things start happening once you've successfully clicked the button at least 5 times. (You'll know you clicked it successfully if it turns light-red. If it doesn't, the click didn't register.) You'll have seen everything there is to see once you've gotten a game-over with more than 30 points.
