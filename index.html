Favorite games

Here are some of my favorite games:

  • Void Stranger
  • Outer Wilds
  • Zero Escape trilogy
  • Celeste
  • Disco Elysium. No link. Pirate it. The dev team literally want you to, the company screwed them over.

Narrative is the most important thing to me in a game. To me, gameplay is also crucial, but primarily because ludonarrative consonance serves the game's narrative. I believe video games as a medium still have a lot of unexplored potential to be developed as an art form and convey strong emotional experiences.

There are also some games I like just for their gameplay, though:

  • Baba Is You
  • Cassette Beasts
  • Stardew Valley

As well as some guilty pleasures, narrativewise, in which cases I'm almost a little bit ashamed to say I think they were good, because the menace that is cringe culture has damaged my enjoyment of them:

  • Omori
  • Undertale
